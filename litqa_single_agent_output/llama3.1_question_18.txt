The disruption of MTF1 expression in Chlamydomonas has a severe effect on the photosynthetic machinery, leading to the depletion of nearly all chloroplast-expressed proteins (Kafri2023 pages 14-14). This is further supported by the observation that most of the chloroplast-expressed proteins are depleted in mtf1 mutants (Kafri2023 pages 44-44). Additionally, genetic rescue experiments showed that alleviating the observed growth defect in the mutant recovered expression of chloroplast-expressed proteins (Kafri2023 pages 14-14).
